## 1. Motivation

## 2. Composition
- What does each instance represent (a question, an activation vector, a token, a prompt-response pair)? ______________________
- How many instances total? What is the unit of scientific interest (qid / group / subject)? ______________________
- What is the label/target, and how is it derived? ______________________

## 3. Collection process
- How was the data acquired (scraped, generated by a model, human-labeled, instrumented from a forward pass)? ______________________
- Over what timeframe was it collected, and does that match the timeframe the claim addresses? ______________________
- What sampling strategy was used (deterministic, probabilistic, convenience)? ______________________
